Interview with James Brown from Non-Zero Sum James

2026-06-08

There is an incredible website that I recommend to students call "Non Zero Sum Games". James Brown does an incredible job writing great posts that are beautifully illustrated on topics of win-win situations. The episodes are usually short delightful and insightful.

I had the privilege of being his first guest interview. You can listen to an episode here.

We talked about a number of things including:

  • the work on the axelrod project (with the upcoming paper on the second tournament)
  • work with Sebastian Krapohl on bilateral trade and with Harry on modelling climate agreements
