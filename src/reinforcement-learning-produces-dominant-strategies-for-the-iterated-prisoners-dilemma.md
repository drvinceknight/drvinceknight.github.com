**Abstract:** We present tournament results and several powerful strategies for the Iterated
Prisoner's Dilemma created using reinforcement learning techniques (evolutionary and
particle swarm algorithms). These strategies are trained to perform well against a
corpus of over 170 distinct opponents, including many well-known and classic strategies.
All the trained strategies win standard tournaments against the total collection of
other opponents. The trained strategies and one particular human made designed strategy
are the top performers in noisy tournaments also.
