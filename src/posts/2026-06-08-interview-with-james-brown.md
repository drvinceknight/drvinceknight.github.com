title: Interview with James Brown from Non-Zero Sum James
---

There is an incredible website that I recommend to students call ["Non Zero Sum
Games"](https://nonzerosum.games). James Brown does an incredible job writing
great posts that are beautifully illustrated on topics of win-win situations.
The episodes are usually short delightful and insightful.

I had the privilege of being his first guest interview. You can listen to an
episode [here](https://pod.link/1810797958/episode/YTQ0MTE4NGItMDA3Zi00NDAyLWJkMjgtZGYyODcyYTFmNzA2).

We talked about a number of things including:

- [the work on the axelrod project](https://axelrod.readthedocs.io/en/stable/)
  (with the upcoming paper on the [second tournament](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.15438))
- work with [Sebastian Krapohl](https://www.uva.nl/en/profile/k/r/s.krapohl/s.krapohl.html)
  on bilateral trade and with [Harry](https://hefos.github.io) on modelling climate agreements
